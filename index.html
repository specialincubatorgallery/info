Our Objectives 我们的目的
To provide a platform for individuals with special needs to develop, cultivate skills, adapt to development, be self-sufficient and learn for life. 为有特殊需要的人提供一个发展的平台，培养技能，适应发展，自给自足，终身学习。
Allows people with special needs to have the opportunity to learn skills for a living, leading to discovering potential, increasing knowledge, cultivating personal confidence, and promoting career development, spontaneously and autonomously. 让有特殊需要的人有机会自动自发和自主地学习生活技能，从而发现潜力，增加知识，培养个人信心，并促进职业发展。
To coordinate and collaborate with the members for the various opportunities in life. 为生活中的各种机会与成员进行协调和合作。
To organise speeches, seminars, and symposiums related to individuals with special needs. 组织与有特殊需要的个人有关的演讲，研讨会和专题讨论会。
To advance handle the business, career and social welfare relating to care for people with special needs. 推动处理与照顾有特殊需要的人有关的业务、职业和社会福利。
To take active parts in international activities related to people with special needs. 积极参加与有特殊需要的人有关的国际活动。
